About Common Snakes Myths

"Their eye shape determines if they're venomous or not"

This is false. Some species of snakes are venomous have round pupils, their eye shape shows the time of day where they are most active.

Do pine needles attract snakes?

This is false. They are not attracted to pine needles, but they may find the pine needles a favorable condition to rest.

Snakes skin are slimy.

This is false. Snake skin is not slimy, it feels more like an old wallet or a smooth basketball.
